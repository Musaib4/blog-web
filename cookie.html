Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how My Blog Web (“we”, “our”, or “the website”) handles cookies and similar technologies. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how your data is used.

No. My Blog Web does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any similar tracking technologies.

2. What This Means for You

Since we do not use cookies:

Our website may contain links to external websites (such as documentation, tools, articles, or resources). These external websites may use their own cookies. We have no control over their cookie policies.

We recommend reviewing the Cookie Policy of any external website you visit.
